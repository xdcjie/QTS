# Risk Model

## Risk categories

- Pre-trade risk
- Intraday risk
- Post-trade risk

## Common rules

- Max order quantity
- Max notional
- Max position
- Max leverage
- Session eligibility
- Restricted instrument list

## Product-specific rules

- Equity: short selling, marginability, concentration, halt/limit rules
- Future: margin, expiry, roll, limit moves
- Option: Greeks, expiry, assignment, naked short restrictions

Risk decisions must be explicit: approved, rejected, or modified. Rejections must include reasons.
